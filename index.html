Visual Font Size Guide

The purpose of this tool is to illustrate how font-faces at various weights and styles render on screen at diffrent sizes.

- The default settings are: sans-serif, capitalized and normal.
- 14px is the baseline font that PT and % sizes are derived form. As it is the common base size for most HTML/Web frameworks.
- 9px is the smallest readble font size online. Anything lower will fail to render correctly in different browsers.

12pt = 14px = 100% — The quick brow fox jumps over the lazy dog.
